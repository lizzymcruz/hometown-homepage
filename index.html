Visit Atok, Benguet

Tucked high up in the Cordillera mountains, Atok is a place where time feels slower, the air is fresher, and the views are bigger than life. From seas of flowers to endless mountain ridges, it’s the kind of destination that makes you stop, breathe, and just enjoy the moment.

Whether you’re here for a short getaway or a full adventure, Atok has something to make you fall in love with the highlands.

Mt. Olis Hike

If you love chasing sunrises or simply want to see the mountains dressed in clouds, Mt. Olis is the place to be. The hike isn’t too tough, and once you reach the top, the view makes every step worth it — think Cordillera mountains stretching endlessly and crisp, cool air that wakes you up better than coffee.

Northern Blossom Flower Farm

This is easily one of Atok’s stars. Imagine rows upon rows of vibrant flowers — celosias, roses, snapdragons — all framed by the rugged beauty of the mountains. It’s the perfect spot for photos, but more than that, it’s a place to just pause, breathe, and enjoy nature’s colors.

The Highest Point (Philippine Highway System)

At 7,400 feet above sea level, this spot along the Halsema Highway gives you a bird’s eye view of Benguet. The pine trees, the winding roads, the endless sky — it’s a reminder of just how high up you are. On a clear day, you can even catch glimpses of nearby peaks.

Best Time to Visit

Atok is beautiful year-round, but January to April is extra special — cooler weather, clear skies, and sometimes even frost in the mornings!

Travel Tips

  • Bring cash (ATMs are scarce).
  • Morning trips are best for clear skies and views.
  • Wear comfy shoes for walking/hiking.
  • Always bring a jacket
